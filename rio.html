Cooperative Maravilha is a group of women residents in the Favela of Providência in the city of Rio de Janeiro. They work in the garment sector, sharing the objective of strengthening their economic activities as social entrepreneurs, aiming purpose, autonomy and income generation. We are supporting this aspiration with legal, educational support and awareness of modern slavery.

I-MiGRa – Migration, Gender and Race Institute is a civil society organization that is born with the premise of respecting what is understood as human rights from a global South perspective, thus serving as a counterpoint to the establishment. The objective is to fight multiple forms of human rights violations with a critical and purposeful perspective, from development of studies and projects to cooperation with public, private and civil society institutions, in order to create durable solutions in the field of Human Rights.
